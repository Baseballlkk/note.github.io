Hola !! Welcome to my note station !!
Here is the host of this station speaking.
This station is open source and free to anyone who is interest in or need knowledge about atmospheric sciences.
I will try my best to provide correct and clear note for courses I've learned.
However, since I mainly study for atmospheric physics, there will be less note for chemistry.
If you are major in or good at atmospheric chemistry,
and also willing to public your note to others,
press "Contact with host " and contact me in email!
It is welcome for everyone to join this project !
